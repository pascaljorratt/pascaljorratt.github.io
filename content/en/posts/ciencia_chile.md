I’d like to share a column published in BioBioChile, in which I discuss with Alonso Carvajal how the quantification of quality and the obsession with numbers has led scientists to resort to practices that are, at the very least, questionable. 
There is a drive to increase the number of points earned at all costs, to the detriment of the quality and integrity of research, **turning researchers into both victims and perpetrators in order to secure their funding.**

You can read the full article [here](https://www.biobiochile.cl/noticias/opinion/tu-voz/2026/03/04/ciencia-en-chile-cuantas-publicaciones-hacen-un-buen-cientifico.shtml).
